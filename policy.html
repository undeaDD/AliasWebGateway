Please note that all event tickets are non-refundable whilst we are operating with the beta version

All fees are not refundable in the circumstance of cancellation of the event. This includes any booking fees and administration fees.

Absence at your booked venue by the date/ time printed on your ticket will be classed as a cancellation by yourself and no refund will be owed to you. Please note, where tickets are non-refundable, the organiser will retain your full booking payment.

Where tickets are non-refundable, this will mean the supplier will retain your full booking payment, including the booking fee, as a cancellation charge. If you do not arrive at your booked venue by the time/date printed on your booking confirmation, this is usually deemed to be a cancellation by you and no refund will be payable to you.
